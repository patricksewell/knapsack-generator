0/1 Knapsack Instance Generator

The 0/1 knapsack problem asks a decision-maker to select a subset of items, each with a price and a value, to maximise total value without exceeding a fixed budget. Each item is either taken or left — hence "0/1." It captures many real-world trade-offs: a consumer choosing which goods to buy given a spending limit, a firm selecting investment projects under a capital constraint, or a government allocating a limited budget across public programmes.
